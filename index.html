About:
Classicircle is a revival of pre-classic minecraft but brought to new heights and is actively being developed by Cyi. You can follow progress NOWHERE as of writing this!

Controls:
WASD to move
Space to jump
R to respawn at spawn point
G to add a mob
Y to swap mouse Y axis
N to generate a new map
Enter to save your map (will let you download the world soon!)
1-9 to change building block type
Left mouse button to remove or add a block
